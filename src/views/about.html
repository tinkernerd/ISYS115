About The Tinkering Nerd

Welcome to the home of Nick Stull, better known as The Tinkering Nerd. This is my space on the web to share ideas, experiments, and creations. Think of it as my Digital Notebook, Second Brain, and Digital Garden rolled into one.

Whether you're exploring polished tutorials, half-baked concepts, or chaotic musings, there's always something interesting growing here. Feel free to dive into the Notes List and see where curiosity takes you.

By the way, I might have ADHD — so expect a delightful array of topics as I follow inspiration wherever it leads. Buckle up; it’s a wild ride!

I’m currently studying Information Technology at Southwestern Michigan College . My passions include programming, home lab tinkering, Raspberry Pi, 3D printing, aviation, networking, and photography. On any given day, you might find me debugging Python scripts, creating Docker containers, or designing something quirky for my D&D games.

This site is a living repository for everything I learn and create. My motto? "Stay curious, stay tinkering." Got questions or ideas? Let’s connect and make something amazing.
